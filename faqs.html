FAQs

We’re here to help

When is the application deadline?

We do not have a set deadline, it’s rolling throughout the year. The best way to apply submit an application through our Apply Form so we can see if we would be a good fit together.

What is the application process?

o Once we receive the pitch deck, the team will access it
o Then if viable, the startup will be put forward to the investment committee
o If scored high enough, the startup will be given to the Board for approval
o When approved they are part of the Catalyst program and ready to receive support and/or funding

Training & Mentorship Schedule?

We do not have a set training program, as the startups have very different needs and are at various stages of development so we find mentors for their specific needs

Can we receive all the funding in one go?

We do not give the funding all at once - we monitor the startups against KPIs and provide the funding in 2-3 tranches

What is the legal agreement between Catalyst and the Startups?

We do a SAFE agreement with convertible note (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)
